Our Project

We strive to create an interactive virtual experience for families transitioning to online therapy prompted by the pandemic. Our one-stop platform connects clinicians to children with developmental disabilities and their caregivers, with a variety of customizable and evidence-based therapeutic activities. Regardless of location, clinicians and caregivers can support children in meeting language and motor development goals in a fun, accessible, and efficient manner.
